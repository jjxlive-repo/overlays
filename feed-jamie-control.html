Auto mode — pick a cumulative counter (total likes or comments since last reset), a target, and an action. Jamie fires the action once the running total reaches the target, with a progress bar on screen the whole way there. Turn on "Auto-escalate" to have it raise the target afterward (add a fixed amount, or multiply) and keep going — e.g. 1000 likes → 2000 → 3000, or 50 comments → 100 → 200.
